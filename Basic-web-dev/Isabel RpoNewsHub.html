A Guy Gets Sent to Jail after Attacking his Wife



Last 12 of December, in Medellin, Colombia, a guy got sent to jail for apparently attacking his 65-year-old wife. He hit his wife with a rock on the back of her head, fracturing her cranium. The man did not take her wife to the hospital, but instead, he applied home medicine, causing the wound to become infected. The woman got sent to the hospital for 45 days and the guy to jail for the rest of his life.
