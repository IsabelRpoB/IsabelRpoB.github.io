Unknown Building's Opening Deleyed due to Legal Problems

Lately, an article has been released informing the sudden construction of an unnamed building in a wealthy part of town. The reporters claim that no citizen knows this building's use or name. All we know is that the building neighbors ain't happy with the construction working hours or the location of the building. But, fortunately for them, the construction is taking a break for a short time due to some possible criminal activity used to pay for the building construction.

A Guy Sent to Jail after Attacking His Wife



Last 12 of December, in Medellin, Colombia, a man got sent to jail for apparently attacking his 65-year-old wife. He hit his wife with a rock on the back of her head, fracturing her cranium. The man did not take her wife to the hospital, but instead, he applied home medicine, causing the wound to become infected. The woman was sent to the hospital for 45 days and the man to jail for the rest of his life.
